About Us

Casa del Pollo is located in Lake Oswego, serving delicious Roast Chicken and Smoked Chicken and other healthy Latin options. As you come in you'll see a spacious dining area, lots of unique art from Costa Rica and will be welcomed by the great aromas of our kitchen.

We offer fresh Costa Rican home style cuisine plus other awesome complimentary items, our meals are light on grease and great on taste. In our cozy dining area you'll find good Latin music in the background, plenty of salsas to choose from at your table and certainly a variety of dishes that will surprise your palate.

In a hurry? Don't worry! Call ahead and we'll have your take out order ready to go.
